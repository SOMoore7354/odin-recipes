Carbonara

Here are the tips on how to make a tasty burrito to make you say, WOW thats a yummy carbonara

    Tortilla
    Rice
    Onion
    Stock Cube
    Beef
Brown the beef over a medium heat in hot oil, then add onions, spices and sugar and cook as per the recipe below. Add tomato puree, stock cub, water and seasoning. Stir and cook on low with the lid on as per the recipe below. Add water if necessary.. Heat the rice, then add them to the warmed tortillas, along with the salsa, beans and cheese. Roll the burritos tightly and serve as they are OR bake in the oven in foil OR fried on both sides on a medium heat.
